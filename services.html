General Dentistry

For all your teeth needs, provided by specialists -

1. Oral Surgery- impacted tooth surgical removal, tooth extraction, atraumatic extraction.

2. Endodontics - root canal treatments, cavity preparation, filling.

3. Prosthodontics - single tooth crown, multi unit bridges , full mouth rehabilitations.

4. Periodontics - flap surgeries, deep scaling, root planing, endo perio lesions.

5. Paedodontics – children’s dental treatment.

The accumulation of plaque and calculus around the teeth and under the gums can cause a cascade of dental problems like tooth decay, gingivitis, bone loss, halitosis (bad breath), and eventually, tooth loss.

We provide a host of services to target these problems. From tooth extraction for impacted teeth to root canals, cavity treatments and full mouth rehabilitation with bridges and dentures.

We also work on various gum diseases, by performing flap surgeries, deep scaling, root planing and endo perio lesions.

Routine cleanings can help rid the mouth of harmful bacteria and track any of these issues before they cause problems. We ensure you can enjoy a lifetime of exceptional oral health.
